Why I'm doing this

There's just no place to get first hand information about Sri Lanka Railways aside from maybe some private forums. For example, if I wanted to get a picture of a specific rail coach, I can't because there's nowhere to get that.

What I will be doing

I will be observing every train I take more than the average person at least. I'm also trying to get my hands on some amazing books about SLR to get more information about what I see.

Who this is for

For those who make 3D models, want to study SLR or just love trains a little too much. Especially those who love model trains but don't have a reliable place to get reference images, dimensions, etc.
