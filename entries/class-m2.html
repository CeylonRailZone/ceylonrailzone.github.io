Class M2 Diesel Locomotive

Updated 2025-09-04

Class M2 is a diesel locomotive made by General Motors Diesel, Canada, imported in the year 1954 under the "Colombo Plan". It's considered one of the most successfull locomotives in Sri Lanka [Wikipedia]
